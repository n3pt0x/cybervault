# 🏢 Corporate Intelligence

> [!NOTE]
> Resources, registries, and tools for investigating companies, their directors, their finances, and their connections.

## 🌍 National Registries

- 🇺🇸 [SEC EDGAR](https://www.sec.gov/edgar.shtml)
- 🇬🇧 [Companies House](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/)
- 🇫🇷 [Societe](https://www.societe.com) / [Infogreffe **(Freemium)**](https://www.infogreffe.fr/)
- 🇩🇪 [Handelsregister](https://www.handelsregister.de/)
- 🇨🇦 [Corporations Canada](https://ised-isde.canada.ca/site/corporations-canada/en)

### 🌐 Multi-country / EU / Global

- [OpenCorporates](https://opencorporates.com/) - Global company registry aggregator. **(Freemium)**
- [European Business Register (EBR)](https://ebr.org/) - Network of official EU business registries. **(Paid)**
- [GLEIF (Global LEI Index)](https://search.gleif.org/#/search/) - Legal Entity Identifiers (LEI) worldwide.

## 👤 Beneficial Ownership & Directors

- [OpenOwnership Register](https://register.openownership.org/) - Global beneficial ownership data.
- [IciJ Offshore Leaks](https://offshoreleaks.icij.org/) - Panama Papers, Paradise Papers.
- [LittleSis](https://littlesis.org/) - US power structure database companies & people.

## 💰 Financial & Sanctions Data

- [OFAC Sanctions List](https://sanctionssearch.ofac.treas.gov/) - US Treasury sanctions.
- [EU Sanctions Map](https://www.sanctionsmap.eu/) - EU sanctions database.
- [World Bank Open Data](https://data.worldbank.org/) - Global financial/economic datasets.
- [IMF Data](https://data.imf.org/) - International Monetary Fund datasets.

## ⚖️ Legal & Compliance

- [CourtListener](https://www.courtlistener.com/) - US court cases and filings.
- [PACER (US)](https://pacer.uscourts.gov/) - Federal court documents. **(Paid)**
- [CaseLaw Access Project](https://case.law/) - US legal cases archive.
- [Legifrance (FR)](https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/) - French legislation & rulings.
