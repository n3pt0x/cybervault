## 🌍 National Registries

- 🇺🇸 [SEC EDGAR](https://www.sec.gov/edgar.shtml) - US company filings (Free)
- 🇬🇧 [Companies House](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/) - UK official registry (Free)
- 🇫🇷 [Infogreffe](https://www.infogreffe.fr/) - French commercial registry (Freemium)
- 🇩🇪 [Handelsregister](https://www.handelsregister.de/) - German company register (Free)
- 🇨🇦 [Corporations Canada](https://ised-isde.canada.ca/site/corporations-canada/en) - Canadian registry (Free)

### 🌐 Multi-country / EU / Global

- [OpenCorporates](https://opencorporates.com/) - Global company registry aggregator (Free / Freemium)
- [European Business Register (EBR)](https://ebr.org/) - Network of official EU business registries (Paid)
- [GLEIF (Global LEI Index)](https://search.gleif.org/#/search/) - Legal Entity Identifiers (LEI) worldwide (Free)
- [EU Transparency Register](https://ec.europa.eu/transparencyregister/public/homePage.do) - Declared lobbies & organizations in EU (Free)

## 👤 Beneficial Ownership & Directors

- [OpenOwnership Register](https://register.openownership.org/) - Global beneficial ownership data. (Free)
- [IciJ Offshore Leaks](https://offshoreleaks.icij.org/) - Panama Papers, Paradise Papers, etc. (Free)
- [LittleSis](https://littlesis.org/) - US power structure database: companies & people (Free)

## 💰 Financial & Sanctions Data

- [OFAC Sanctions List](https://sanctionssearch.ofac.treas.gov/) - US Treasury sanctions (Free)
- [EU Sanctions Map](https://www.sanctionsmap.eu/) - EU sanctions database (Free)
- [World Bank Open Data](https://data.worldbank.org/) - Global financial/economic datasets (Free)
- [IMF Data](https://data.imf.org/) - International Monetary Fund datasets (Free)

## ⚖️ Legal & Compliance

- [CourtListener](https://www.courtlistener.com/) - US court cases and filings (Free)
- [PACER (US)](https://pacer.uscourts.gov/) - Federal court documents (Paid)
- [CaseLaw Access Project](https://case.law/) - US legal cases archive (Free)
- [Legifrance (FR)](https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/) - French legislation & rulings (Free)
