# 📍 Geolocation & Tracking

## 🗺️ Maps & Geolocation

- [Overpass Turbo](https://overpass-turbo.eu/) - Advanced queries on OpenStreetMap.
- [GPS Coordinates](https://www.gps-coordinates.net/) - Convert and find GPS coordinates.
- [Mapillary](https://www.mapillary.com/) - Collaborative street-level imagery.
- [Sentinel Hub Playground](https://apps.sentinel-hub.com/sentinel-playground/) - Free satellite imagery explorer.
- [Zoom Earth](https://zoom.earth/) - Real-time satellite images, weather, and storms.
- [Wikimapia](http://wikimapia.org/) - User-contributed geospatial map.
- [PeakVisor](https://peakvisor.com/) - Identify mountains and landscapes in photos.

### 🤖 AI GeoSpy

- [geospy.net](https://geospy.net) - AI tool for location discovery from images.
- [geospy.ai](https://app.geospy.ai/) - AI tool for location discovery from images.

## ✈️ Transport & Tracking (Flights / Ships / Trains)

- [skytrack (Github)](https://github.com/ANG13T/skytrack)
- [FlightRadar24](https://www.flightradar24.com/) - Live flight tracking.
- [ADS-B Exchange](https://globe.adsbexchange.com/) - Unfiltered global flight tracking.
- [RadarBox](https://www.radarbox.com/) - Aircraft and airspace monitoring.
- [MarineTraffic](https://www.marinetraffic.com/) - Live ship and vessel tracking.
- [VesselFinder](https://www.vesselfinder.com/) - Global ship tracking and databases.
- [Raildar](https://raildar.co.uk/) - UK train live tracking.
- [OpenRailwayMap](https://www.openrailwaymap.org/) - Railway infrastructure worldwide.

## 🛰️ Satellite & Visual Intelligence

- [Google Earth Engine](https://earthengine.google.com/) - Geospatial big data analysis.
- [Sentinel Hub EO Browser](https://apps.sentinel-hub.com/eo-browser/) - Explore and download satellite imagery.
- [NASA Worldview](https://worldview.earthdata.nasa.gov/) - Interactive satellite data from NASA.
- [SkyMap](https://stellarium.org/) - Identify celestial objects (astronomy-based geoloc).
- [Terraserver](https://www.terraserver.com/) - Satellite imagery and aerial photos.
- [Soar.Earth](https://soar.earth/) - Community-driven maps and satellite imagery.
- [In-The-Sky.org Satellites](https://in-the-sky.org/satmap_worldmap.php) - Real-time satellite tracking.

## 📡 Wi-Fi / Network Geolocation (OSINT)

- [Wigle.net](https://wigle.net/) - Collaborative database of Wi-Fi access points with geolocation. Useful for mapping networks and identifying locations.
- [OpenWiFiMap](https://openwifimap.net/) - Community-driven map of Wi-Fi hotspots worldwide.
- [WiGLE API](https://api.wigle.net/) - Programmatic access to Wigle.net database for OSINT projects.
- [Wi-Fi Map](https://www.wifi-map.com/) - Global map of public Wi-Fi networks shared by users.
- [Mozilla Location Service](https://location.services.mozilla.com/) - Open API providing geolocation information based on Wi-Fi networks.

## 🛠️ Identificator Tools

### 🌐 Geo & Object Identifiers

- [GeoHack](https://geohack.toolforge.org/) - Convert coordinates, map tools, and references.
- [What3Words](https://what3words.com/) - Precise location identification using 3-word addresses.
- [Google Lens](https://lens.google/) - Identify objects, places, and landmarks via images.

### 🚗 Vehicles

- [Carnet.ai](https://carnet.ai/) - Identify car models from photos.
- [VinDecoder.net](https://www.vindecoder.net/) - Decode VIN numbers for vehicles.
- [Car2DB](https://voiture.car2db.com/) - Retrieve technical characteristics of cars.

### 🌞 Sun & Light

- [SunCalc](https://www.suncalc.org/#/27.6936,-97.5195,3/2025.08.29/15:21/1/3) - Sun position calculator.
- [Time and Date Sun Calculator](https://www.timeanddate.com/sun/) - Sunrise, sunset, daylight duration for any location.

### 🔌 Electronics

- [World Plugs](https://www.iec.ch/world-plugs) - Identify electrical plug types worldwide.

### 🌱 Plants

- [PlantNet](https://identify.plantnet.org/en) - Identify plants via image recognition.
